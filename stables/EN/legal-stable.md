# Legal Stable

## Problem

Legal reasoning requires careful interpretation of rules, obligations, and constraints.

Legal discussions typically involve:

- contractual obligations
- regulatory requirements
- liability considerations
- interpretation of formal language

Unlike exploratory discussions, legal analysis must maintain **precision and traceability** in how rules and interpretations are applied.

Small shifts in wording or interpretation can significantly change the meaning of a clause or obligation.

AI collaboration introduces additional risk in legal reasoning.  
Language models often generate confident interpretations even when the legal context is incomplete or ambiguous.

As a result, discussions may gradually introduce assumptions, reinterpret legal language, or blur the distinction between interpretation and speculation.

Over time, this can lead to incorrect conclusions about obligations, permissions, or risks.

Legal Stable addresses this problem by enforcing a **rule-grounded legal reasoning workflow**.

---

## Failure Pattern

Typical failures observed during AI-assisted legal analysis include:

- interpreting legal text without identifying the governing rule
- mixing interpretation with speculation
- overlooking relevant constraints or exceptions
- rephrasing legal language in ways that alter its meaning
- treating hypothetical reasoning as established interpretation
- drawing conclusions without tracing them back to a specific rule or clause

These behaviors destabilize legal reasoning because conclusions must remain grounded in explicit legal sources.

---

## What This Mode Does

Legal Stable introduces an operational discipline focused on **rule-grounded interpretation**.

Instead of allowing natural language reasoning to freely reinterpret legal concepts, the mode anchors discussion to identifiable legal sources.

The workflow emphasizes three structural references:

- governing rules or clauses
- **interpretive boundaries**
- potential legal implications

By maintaining these anchors, Legal Stable prevents discussions from drifting into unsupported interpretation.

The mode prioritizes **traceable reasoning over narrative explanation**, ensuring that conclusions are explicitly connected to the rules or clauses that justify them.

---

## Expected Improvements

Using Legal Stable improves collaboration stability by:

- maintaining traceability between conclusions and legal sources
- preventing speculative interpretations
- preserving the meaning of formal legal language
- clarifying obligations, permissions, and restrictions
- reducing ambiguity in legal reasoning

As a result, legal analysis becomes more transparent and easier to verify.

---

## Operational Structure

The reasoning workflow enforced by Legal Stable follows a structured legal analysis sequence.

Step 1 — Identify the governing rule  
Determine the relevant law, regulation, or contractual clause.

Step 2 — Interpret the rule  
Clarify what the rule requires or permits.

Step 3 — Apply the rule to the situation  
Evaluate how the rule affects the specific scenario.

Step 4 — Consider constraints and exceptions  
Identify conditions that may limit or modify the rule.

Step 5 — Derive conclusions  
Form conclusions that remain traceable to the governing rule.

This structure keeps legal reasoning grounded in identifiable sources and prevents unsupported interpretation.
